Quality Assurance

Manage dataset QA workflows, inter-annotator agreement, and performance metrics.

All three filters are multi-select — check as many options as you need. Options are ordered:

  1. Status — Kanban column order (Assigned → Finalized)
  2. Detection — alphabetical
  3. Assigned To — alphabetical, all annotators

The Kanban and List views both respect the filters — try selecting In QA to narrow the board.

Kanban view is the default — one column per QA status, drag cards between columns to move a dataset forward or back in the pipeline.

List view collapses the same datasets into a scannable table.

Cards route to different tabs when clicked, based on their status:

  1. Assigned / In Annotation / Needs Revision → Annotation tab (opens the dataset)
  2. Submitted / In QA → QA Sampling subtab
  3. Approved / Finalized → Datasets tab

You can also drag a card between columns to move the dataset forward or back in the pipeline. Try dragging one now — invalid transitions will be rejected.
